# Provo Body Shop — Estimate & Invoice Tool

Estimator tool for my father's body shop. Generate professional estimates, invoices, and insurance bills.

## Live App

https://danielgarylawrence123-jpg.github.io/Provo-body-shop/

## Features

- Estimate & Invoice generator with multi-vehicle support
- Insurance Bill section — bill insurance companies for multiple vehicles at once
- Parts & labor line items with auto-calculated totals
- Export to PDF, JPG, or Print
- Bilingual support (English, Spanish, Haitian Creole)
- Customer history saved locally in browser
